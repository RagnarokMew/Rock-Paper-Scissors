The duel before the throne...

In the middle of the night, the king hands down your sentence for crimes you did not commit. The sentence is death for your sins. You already know the champion of the kingdom framed you for everything so you won't ascend through the ranks and take his place.

In order to save yourself, you challenge the champion to a duel before the throne, according to the Triple Church of the Rock, Paper and Scissors. The winner would be absolved of any crimes, while the loser would be executed on the spot. This is your chance to clear your to clear your name and bring judgement down on the one undeserving of the title of champion.

The game functions just like Rock Paper Scissors. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper and Paper beats Rock.
